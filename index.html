Core Values

SERVE
The Student Activities and Development Unit focuses on cultivating a community of leaders who are passionate, proactive, innovative, and dedicated to serving others. With this in mind, every student leader is encouraged to embrace the belief that giving is more valuable than receiving—a principle that forms the foundation of leadership.


LEAD
The Student Activities and Development Unit strives to develop student leaders who are not only good followers but also confident decision-makers who take initiative in every situation they encounter. Through comprehensive training programs, student leaders are equipped with the skills needed to face real-life challenges and lead effectively.


EXCEL
Excellence is a fundamental value that FEU Diliman student leaders are expected to embody in both their academic and personal lives. This commitment to excellence is a defining trait of every iTamaraw. The Student Activities and Development Unit supports this pursuit of excellence by offering a variety of training programs and developmental activities that prepare student leaders to succeed in every aspect of life.

About Us

The Student Activities and Development Unit oversees student-led initiatives that promote leadership, creativity, and excellence. We ensure proper coordination between student organizations and their advisers for efficient activity planning and execution.
