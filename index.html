Core Values

SERVE
The Student Activities and Development Unit focuses on cultivating a community of leaders who are passionate, proactive, innovative, and dedicated to serving others.


LEAD
The Student Activities and Development Unit strives to develop student leaders who are confident decision-makers and proactive in every situation.


EXCEL
Excellence is a defining trait of every student leader. The unit provides training and developmental activities to support this pursuit of excellence.

About Us

The Student Activities and Development Unit (SADU) at FEU Diliman is dedicated to nurturing student leaders and supporting dynamic student organizations. We serve as the backbone of all student-led initiatives, providing guidance and approval for activity proposals that enrich the campus experience.
